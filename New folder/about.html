Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA)

Your Pathway to a Successful Business Career

Specializations Offered

  • Entrepreneurship – Fosters creativity and innovation, equipping students to launch and manage sustainable businesses.
  • Business Management – Prepares students for leadership roles by emphasizing strategic planning and organizational development.
  • Marketing Management – Provides expertise in market analysis, brand management, and digital marketing strategies.
  • Financial Management – Develops proficiency in financial planning, investment analysis, and risk management.
  • Operations Management – Focuses on optimizing business processes and supply chain management.
  • Human Resource Management – Equips students with skills to manage talent acquisition and employee relations.
  • Business Economics – Combines economic theory with business practices to guide strategic decision-making.
  • Business Accounting and Auditing – Develops expertise in financial reporting and auditing standards.

Sample Course Structure

  • Year 1: Introduction to Business, Principles of Management, Financial Accounting
  • Year 2: Business Communication, Marketing Principles, Microeconomics
  • Year 3: Organizational Behavior, Business Ethics, Operations Management
  • Year 4: Strategic Management, Business Policy, Capstone Project

Career Opportunities

  • Business Analyst
  • Marketing Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • Human Resource Specialist
  • Operations Manager
  • Entrepreneur
