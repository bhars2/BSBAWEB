How to Enroll in BSBA at SMIT Tacurong

Enrollment Steps

  1. Prepare the required documents (see below).
  2. Visit the Admissions Office at SMIT Tacurong.
  3. Submit your documents for evaluation.
  4. Pay the entrance fee at the Accounting Office.
  5. Complete any additional assessments or interviews if required.
  6. Receive confirmation of your enrollment.

Required Documents

  • Form 138 (High School Report Card)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character from your previous school
  • PSA Birth Certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Vaccination Card/Certificate (photocopy)
